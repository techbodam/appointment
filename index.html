What types of MRI scans do you offer?

We offer both 1.5 Tesla and 3 Tesla MRI scans, covering a wide range of diagnostic needs including brain, spine, joints, abdomen, and full body scans. Our state-of-the-art equipment ensures high-quality imaging for accurate diagnosis.

How long does an MRI scan take?

The duration of an MRI scan typically ranges from 20 to 45 minutes, depending on the type of scan and area being examined. We strive to maintain efficiency while ensuring comprehensive imaging.

Do I need a doctor's referral for an MRI scan?

Yes, we require a doctor's referral or prescription for MRI scans to ensure the examination is medically necessary and to properly focus on the area of concern.

How soon can I get my MRI results?

We provide quick turnaround times for results, typically within 24-48 hours after the scan. For urgent cases, we can expedite the reporting process upon request.

What should I wear for an MRI scan?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothes without any metal components. We provide hospital gowns if needed. Please remove all jewelry, watches, and metal objects before the scan.

What are your payment options?

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit cards, and major insurance plans. We also offer flexible payment plans for patients who need financial assistance.
